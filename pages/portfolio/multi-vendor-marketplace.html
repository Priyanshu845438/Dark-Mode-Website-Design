Project Overview

MarketConnect Inc required a robust multi-vendor e-commerce platform to compete with major marketplace players. The platform needed to support over 10,000 vendors, process millions of transactions monthly, and provide real-time inventory synchronization across multiple channels.

Our solution delivered a scalable marketplace that increased sales by 240% within six months, reduced transaction processing time by 60%, and achieved a 98.5% customer satisfaction rate. The platform now handles over $50M in annual transactions with zero downtime during peak sales periods.
