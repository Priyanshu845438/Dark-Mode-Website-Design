Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our mobile app development services

Which mobile platforms do you develop for?

We develop for both iOS and Android platforms using native development (Swift/Kotlin) and cross-platform solutions (React Native, Flutter).

How long does mobile app development take?

Mobile app development typically takes 8-16 weeks depending on complexity, features, and platforms. Simple apps take 8-12 weeks, complex apps 12-24 weeks.

Do you help with app store submission?

Yes, we handle the complete app store submission process including Apple App Store and Google Play Store, ensuring compliance with all guidelines.

What's the difference between native and cross-platform apps?

Native apps offer better performance and platform-specific features but require separate development. Cross-platform apps share codebase but may have slight performance differences.

Do you provide post-launch app maintenance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive post-launch support including bug fixes, feature updates, performance optimization, and compatibility updates for new OS versions.
