Security Policy

Responsible Vulnerability Disclosure

Our Commitment to Security

At Acadify Solution, we take the security of our systems and our clients' data seriously. We appreciate the efforts of security researchers and the broader community in helping us maintain the highest security standards.

Reporting Security Vulnerabilities

How to Report

  • Email: security@acadifysolution.com
  • Contact Form: Contact Us (mark as "Security Issue")
  • Encryption: PGP key available at acadifysolution.com/pgp-key.txt

What to Include

  • Detailed description of the vulnerability
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • Potential impact assessment
  • Your contact information for follow-up
  • Any proof-of-concept code (if applicable)

Our Response Process

24h

Acknowledgment

We will acknowledge receipt of your report within 24 hours.

72h

Initial Assessment

We will provide an initial assessment within 72 hours.

7d

Regular Updates

We will provide regular updates on our investigation progress.

30d

Resolution

We aim to resolve critical issues within 30 days.

Recognition Program

We believe in recognizing security researchers who help us improve our security posture:

  • Hall of Fame: Public acknowledgment on our security acknowledgments page
  • Coordinated Disclosure: We work with researchers on responsible disclosure timelines
  • Credit: Proper attribution for valid vulnerability reports

Scope and Guidelines

In Scope

  • acadifysolution.com and all subdomains
  • Web applications and APIs
  • Client-side vulnerabilities (XSS, CSRF, etc.)
  • Server-side vulnerabilities (SQL injection, RCE, etc.)
  • Authentication and authorization flaws

Out of Scope

  • Third-party services we don't control
  • Social engineering attacks
  • Physical security issues
  • Denial of service attacks
  • Spam or phishing attacks

Responsible Disclosure Guidelines

  • Do not access, modify, or delete data belonging to others
  • Do not perform attacks that could harm the availability of our services
  • Do not disclose the vulnerability publicly until we've had a chance to fix it
  • Make a good faith effort to avoid privacy violations and data destruction

Security Standards

We maintain security through:

  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Secure coding practices and code reviews
  • Employee security training and awareness
  • Incident response procedures
  • Data encryption in transit and at rest
  • Regular security updates and patches
