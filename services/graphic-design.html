Design Process

Our Graphic Design Journey

We follow a collaborative design process that ensures every graphic we create aligns with your brand vision and business objectives. From concept to final delivery, we work closely with you to bring your ideas to life.

01

Discovery & Brief

1-2 Days

We start by understanding your brand, target audience, and design requirements. Our team gathers detailed project specifications and creative direction through comprehensive briefing sessions.

Key Deliverables:

  • Creative Brief Document
  • Brand Guidelines Analysis
  • Target Audience Profile
  • Project Timeline & Scope
02

Concept Development

3-5 Days

Our creative team develops initial concepts and design directions. We create multiple design options that explore different visual approaches while staying true to your brand identity.

Key Deliverables:

  • Initial Concept Sketches
  • Mood Boards & Inspiration
  • Color Palette Options
  • Typography Selections
03

Design & Refinement

5-7 Days

We create polished designs based on approved concepts and incorporate your feedback through iterative refinement. Our team ensures every detail meets professional standards.

Key Deliverables:

  • High-Fidelity Design Mockups
  • Multiple Format Variations
  • Brand Application Examples
  • Revision Cycles
04

Finalization & Delivery

1-2 Days

We prepare final files in all required formats and provide comprehensive brand guidelines. Our team ensures you have everything needed for successful implementation.

Key Deliverables:

  • Final Design Files (All Formats)
  • Brand Usage Guidelines
  • Source Files & Assets
  • Implementation Support
